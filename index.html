About scinchan cartoon

brief information

An anime adaptation of Crayon Shin-chan, produced by Shin-Ei Animation, has aired in Japan on TV Asahi since April 13, 1992. The series was originally directed by Mitsuru Hongo from 1992 to 1996, and was replaced by Keiichi Hara from 1996 to 2004. Since 2004, the series is directed by Yuji Muto.

Devara movie

Devara: Part 1 is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Koratala Siva. Produced by Yuvasudha Arts and N. T. R. Arts, the film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. in the titular role alongside Saif Ali Khan, Prakash Raj and Janhvi Kapoor (in her Telugu debut).
